About the Library Seat Reservation System

The Library Seat Reservation System is a web-based platform that allows library users to easily check available seats, reserve them for a specified period, and view the list of books available in the library.

Our Purpose

Our goal is to provide a seamless, efficient experience for students, researchers, and library visitors by ensuring they can easily reserve a seat in the library, access reading materials, and manage their time effectively.

Features

  • Real-time Seat Availability: See the current status of seats in various areas.
  • Easy Booking: Quick and easy reservation system with an option to cancel or modify bookings.
  • Book Information: Get details of books available in the library, including titles, authors, and availability.
  • User-Friendly Interface: The system is simple and intuitive to navigate.

Instructions

To ensure a smooth and efficient experience for everyone using the Library Seat Reservation System, please adhere to the following rules:

  • Booking Limit: You can reserve a seat for up to 4 hours per session.
  • Cancelations: Please cancel your reservation at least 2 hours before the reserved time if you no longer need the seat.
  • Timely Arrival: Arrive at your reserved seat within 15 minutes of the start time. Late arrivals may lose their reservation.
  • Respect Other Users: Please maintain quiet behavior and avoid disturbing others while using the library.
  • Personal Belongings: Personal items should not occupy multiple seats.
